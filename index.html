QuANTs

The purpose of QuANTs is to provide quality assurance and assessment for Advanced Normalization Tools (ANTs). Specifically, routines will be provided to evaluate the output of scripts inlcluded with ANTs such as antsCorticalThickness and antsNeuroimagingBattery. A two-layer approach will be taken:

  • Cluster based generatation of csv files and images for individual data sets
  • Generation of summary csv files, graph and images for large sets of data (studies, etc)
